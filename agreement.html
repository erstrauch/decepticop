Study background

  • We suspect that heavy gamers have cardiovascular heart problems at higher rates than the general population.
  • Studies show that intense gameplay can be a stressor for those with heart conditions.
  • Heartbeat often manifests irregularly in those with heart conditions during intense gameplay.
  • Undiagnosed heart conditions may cause some at-risk players to experience adverse health effects.
  • To address this, we are developing a lightweight system which performs Eularian Video Magnification, a video processing method, and can remotely infer heart rate using a webcam.

Partipation role

  • In this experiment, you are in the control group (non-heavy gamer).
  • We will record your face while you play the card game, "BS" against a computer.

Instructions
